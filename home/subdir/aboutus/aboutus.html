About us

AAEWS is a social welfare organisation which provide awareness about Education & Literacy, Health & Family Welfare.

We accomplish this by providing online and offline resources to increase their awareness, and skills. We use technology to spread knowledge from our experienced social experts.

We empower children, youth, and families to effectively navigate through change,We conduct regular, consistent support to those in need.

What we do

We do free Health checkup in rural area, conduct programe about financial education with the help of government and locals.
